About

  • High Quality

    Color Ice Australia is a high-quality production company, specialising in Ice Shows. Color Ice Australia is a new branch of the original Color Ice company based in Russia and has a 14 years history of performing around the world.

  • Successful

    We are a hugely successful ice-theatre company, staging many productions around Russia, Europe, Asia and Australia. Our productions are wide-ranging and versatile, and we can create shows for small local ice rinks, to beautiful theatres, to giant stadiums. Our company strives to provide the best level of ice skating and entertainment.

  • International

    We collect the best skaters from all around the world and choose the most professional choreographers as well as colourful costumes and decorations to make sure that our customers and viewers will have the best experience and memories of each performance.

  • Fast-growing

    We are fast-growing and ambitious company and we are looking forward to impressing you in future with new shows and ideas!!
